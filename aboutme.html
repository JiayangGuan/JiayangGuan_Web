About Jiayang Guan

Jiayang Guan (Jay), a 20-year-old male college student, is currently in his third year at BMCC, majoring in studio art. Originally from China, he has been residing in New York for 7 years and currently resides in Brooklyn. Jay's passion lies in drawing and playing games, which he can do for hours on end. He describes himself as a reserved individual, typically speaking only when necessary. Jay's interests include e-sports, motorsports, and a love for both pop and symphony music. Magenta and purple are his favorite colors. His current objective is to transfer to Hunter College, where he hopes to pursue his dream career as a video game concept artist.
