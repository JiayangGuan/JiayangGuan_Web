Horn is not the way to vent your anger

I created a fully animated and hand-drawn short film entirely on my own. The minimalistic style was adapted due to time constraints. This captivating story revolves around a driver who encounters persistent honking from the car behind him in various scenarios. Eventually, the driver fails to notice pedestrians on the sidewalk and ends up in a car accident. The film serves as a powerful reminder to practice patience while driving. It's important to note that the plot is entirely my original concept, any resemblance to real-life events is purely coincidental.
